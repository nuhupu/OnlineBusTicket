About SRC Travel Agencies

SRC Travel Agencies is the online bus booking website that offers sightseeing bus, expressway bus from Ho Chi Minh City to City Vung Tau. Moreover, registered bus companies on SRC Travel Agencies are all authorized by the Ministry of Land, Infrastructure, Transport and Tourism, offering the highest level of comfort and safety.

Our company, Team4 launched the Vietnamese website SRC Travel Agencies which offers online bus booking service in 2016. Our registered bus companies are more than 120 ,and we provide multiple bus routes for customers, which makes us the biggest online bus booking website in VietNam. The number of customers who registered with SRC Travel Agencies has reached more than 4,000,000.

The number of foreigners who visit VietNam has been increasing, and approximately 18,000,000 foreigners visited City Ho Chi Minh and City Vung Tau in 2015 according to VietNam National Tourism Organization. We have set up an online bus booking website in English ,"SRC Travel Agencies" in 2015 ,hoping you can travel to your destination at ease with no hassle. We are pleased to offer you a great travel experience and satisfaction at reasonable prices. We hope that we can be of your help to make your journey more comfortable.
